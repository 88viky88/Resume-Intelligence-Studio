The 5-Step Workflow:
Upload Resume — paste text or drag-and-drop a .txt/.md file, with optional target role
First-Pass Analysis — AI scores the resume (0–100), lists strengths, gaps, and flags 13 missing area categories with color-coded severity tags
Add Missing Details — personalized follow-up questions auto-generated per company (e.g. "At Confluent, what KPIs improved?"), with skip checkboxes and grouped by employer
Re-Analysis — before/after score comparison with point delta, improvement list, and new bullet points generated strictly from your answers
Optimized Resume — full rewritten resume merged from original + answers, ATS-friendly, impact-driven, with copy and download buttons
